Sesame Matcha

Description

Grassy matcha and toasty sesame froth into nature in a cup. Smooth and subtly sweet, it's the kind of drink that feels grounding, luxurious, and a little unexpected.

Ingredients

  • Black Sesame Paste
    • 1 tbsp black sesame paste (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1–2 tsp honey or maple syrup (to taste)
  • Matcha Layer
    • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder (ceremonial grade preferred)
    • 1–2 tbsp hot water (not boiling, ~175°F / 80°C)
  • Milk Layer
    • 3/4 cup milk of choice (oat, almond, dairy, soy — all work)
    • Ice cubes (if serving cold)
    • Optional: Sesame seeds for garnish

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Sesame Paste:
    • In your serving mug, stir the sesame paste with honey or maple syrup until smooth.
  2. Whisk the Matcha:
    • Sift the matcha powder into a small bowl.
    • Add hot water and whisk with a bamboo whisk (chasen) or small frother until frothy and smooth.
  3. Assemble the Drink:
    • Pour the warm milk into the mug with sesame paste and stir well to combine.
    • Slowly pour the whisked matcha on top for a layered effect, or stir fully for a uniform green-gray blend.
    • Optional: sprinkle with black or white sesame seeds for garnish.
  4. Serve:
    • Stir before drinking for the perfect blend of flavors.
    • Optional: Top with whipped cream or a sprinkle of crushed freeze-dried strawberries for flair.
