Strawberry Matcha Latte

Description

A strawberry matcha latte is exactly as it sounds: a delicious fusion of sweet strawberries and earthy matcha! Perfect over ice for a refreshing yet cozy treat :>

Ingredients

  • Strawberry Layer
    • 4–5 fresh strawberries (or 1/3 cup frozen, thawed)
    • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tbsp water (optional, for blending)
  • Matcha Layer
    • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder (ceremonial grade preferred)
    • 1–2 tbsp hot water (not boiling, ~175°F / 80°C)
  • Milk Layer
    • 3/4 cup milk of choice (oat, almond, dairy, soy — all work)
    • Ice cubes (if serving cold)

Recipe

  1. Make the Strawberry Purée:
    • In a small blender or bowl, combine strawberries, honey/sugar, and water.
    • Blend or mash until smooth and syrupy.
    • Pour into the bottom of your glass.
  2. Whisk the Matcha:
    • Sift the matcha powder into a small bowl.
    • Add hot water and whisk with a bamboo whisk (chasen) or small frother until frothy and smooth.
  3. Assemble the Drink:
    • Add ice over the strawberry layer if making an iced version.
    • Slowly pour in the milk to create a white middle layer.
    • Gently pour the whisked matcha over the back of a spoon to float it on top.
  4. Serve:
    • Stir before drinking for the perfect blend of flavors.
    • Optional: Top with whipped cream or a sprinkle of crushed freeze-dried strawberries for flair.
